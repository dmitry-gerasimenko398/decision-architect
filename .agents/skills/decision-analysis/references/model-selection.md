# Model selection

Choose a model from decision structure, not topic words.

## Select `sequential_exploration`

Use it when the choice repeats over a finite horizon, trying a new option produces information, a good discovery can be reused, and the current decision is explore versus exploit. Do not use it merely because a choice happens more than once; reusable information is essential.

## Select `multi_criteria`

Use it for one choice among 2–4 known alternatives evaluated through several compensable preferences and possibly mandatory constraints.

## Clarify once when needed

Ask:

> Will you make this choice only once, or will you have several future opportunities to try new options and reuse the best one you discover?

Explain the selection in one sentence. Stop classifying once the structure is clear.

## Reject an unsupported fit

Do not force either model onto continuous optimization, portfolio allocation, negotiation, voting, open-ended planning, or a repeated choice where exploration produces no reusable information. Explain which structural requirement is missing.
